# Mentorship Call: Love as Foundation for Leadership
**Date:** None
**Attendees:** Curtis Gilbert, Mentor/Pastor

## Executive Summary
A mentorship conversation focused on spiritual leadership principles, emphasizing that love is the foundational requirement for effective ministry. The mentor shared a formative story about a student named Avery (2004) to illustrate how genuine love transforms relationships and outcomes. Key message: perfectionism and fear of failure are obstacles; showing up with love and surrendering personal agenda to God's will is what matters. The mentor encouraged Curtis to embrace mistakes as part of God's plan and to lead the team by prioritizing God's agenda first, then extending love to others in unity.

## Key Decisions
- Adopt 'love first' as the primary leadership principle for ministry
- Release perfectionism and fear of mistakes as impediments to God's work
- Prioritize God's agenda over personal concerns when leading
