## HOW TO READ THIS DOCUMENT

Each revenue stream is broken into:
- **The Offer** — what exactly you're selling and why someone buys it
- **The Buyer** — who they are, their pain, their desire
- **Funnel Map** — how they go from stranger → customer → repeat buyer
- **Implementation Steps** — numbered, sequential. Curtis's jobs vs. J5's jobs are explicitly labeled.
- **Launch Timeline** — what happens when
- **Success Metrics** — how you know it's working
- **Interdependencies** — what this stream feeds or requires from other streams

**Revenue streams are ordered by priority and feasibility — start at #1.**
