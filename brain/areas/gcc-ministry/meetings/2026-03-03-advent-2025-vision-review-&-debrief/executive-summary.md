# Advent 2025 Vision Review & Debrief
**Date:** None
**Attendees:** Curtis Gilbert, Melissa, Natalie, Jess, Rich Mayo

## Executive Summary
Curtis led a comprehensive debrief of Garden City's first Advent season, reviewing alignment with the vision that 'Jesus weaves our individual stories into one flourishing narrative.' The team evaluated how creative and logistical elements (decor, communications, storytelling, prayer/care) reflected the core desired outcomes: helping people recognize they're part of God's story, increasing cross-generational connection, and creating a warm but sustainable experience. Key strengths included cohesive creativity, potent family story cards, and successful website integration. The team identified opportunities for more intentional connection between prayer moments and the broader Advent narrative for future years.

## Key Decisions
- Story cards will be preserved and built upon for future Advent seasons rather than discarded
- Approach to notes: capture general themes and action items rather than verbatim transcription
- Future focus on more intentional connection between prayer/candle lighting moments and the broader Advent story arc
