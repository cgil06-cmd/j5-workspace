## Captured from Curtis's session, 2026-02-28

## CORE PHILOSOPHY
**The "Both/And" Brand:** Every piece of content reinforces the both/and nature of human experience.
- Anxious AND Faithful
- Broken AND Beloved
- Flawed AND Flourishing

**Authenticity First:** Never share a success story without sharing the struggle that birthed it.

---

## CONTENT PILLARS
1. **Authenticity First** — struggle before success, always
2. **The "And" Frequency** — both/and framing in every piece of content
